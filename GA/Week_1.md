# Graded Assignment 1

## 1. “Unbiased opinion” is an example of _____.

- a) Oxymoron
- b) Paradox
- c) Antithesis
- d) None of the above

✅ **Accepted Answer:** a) Oxymoron

---

## 2. “Magical realism” is an example of paradox. This statement is _____.

- a) True
- b) False

✅ **Accepted Answer:** b) False

---

## 3. Identify the figure of speech of the underlined word in the following sentence.

**“Friends, Romans, Countrymen, lend me your ears”**

- a) Synecdoche
- b) Metonymy
- c) Metaphor
- d) Irony

✅ **Accepted Answer:** b) Metonymy

---

## 4. _____, he was present at the venue during the incident.

- a) Nevertheless
- b) Initially
- c) Apparently
- d) Firstly

✅ **Accepted Answer:** c) Apparently

---

## 5. Which of the following discourse markers are used to express negative opinion?

- a) To be honest
- b) Honestly
- c) In my opinion
- d) Both (a) and (b)

✅ **Accepted Answer:** d) Both (a) and (b)

---

## 6. It’s completely booked. ___ you are not interested in the show.

- a) Apparently
- b) Even though
- c) Anyway
- d) But

✅ **Accepted Answer:** c) Anyway

---

## 7. Which of the following discourse marker is used to express an opposing idea?

- a) Apparently
- b) However
- c) Honestly
- d) None of the above

✅ **Accepted Answer:** b) However

---

## 8. ____ being a dancer, she is a good writer.

- a) Actually
- b) Besides
- c) Instead
- d) In addition

✅ **Accepted Answer:** b) Besides

---

## 9. Intonation is responsible for variations in _____.

- a) Rhythm
- b) Stress
- c) Accent
- d) Pitch

✅ **Accepted Answer:** d) Pitch

---

## 10. Intonation is not visible in the written language. This statement is _____.

- a) True
- b) False

✅ **Accepted Answer:** a) True
