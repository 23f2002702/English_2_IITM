# Graded Assignment 2

## 1. Identify the adverb in the following sentence.

**Seetha is listening patiently.**

- a) Listening
- b) My
- c) Patiently
- d) Is listening patiently

✅ **Accepted Answer:** c) Patiently

---

## 2. Identify the adverb in the following sentence.

**My dog is not sleeping.**

- a) Is not
- b) Is not sleeping
- c) Not
- d) My

✅ **Accepted Answer:** c) Not

---

## 3. Tense denotes:

- a) State of the event
- b) Time of the event
- c) Form of the verb
- d) None of the above

✅ **Accepted Answer:** b) Time of the event

---

## 4. Intransitive verbs cannot be recast in passive form.

- a) True
- b) False

✅ **Accepted Answer:** a) True

---

## 5. Complete the following sentence with the most appropriate word.

**She bought an _______ sound system.**

- a) Food
- b) Expensive
- c) Cheap
- d) Expenditure

✅ **Accepted Answer:** b) Expensive

---

## 6. Complete the following sentence with the most appropriate word.

**My cat is sleeping on the _____ roof.**

- a) Think
- b) Thinly
- c) Thin
- d) Understanding

✅ **Accepted Answer:** c) Thin

---

## 7. ‘________, I never believed in the rumours.’

- a) Frankly
- b) Honestly
- c) To be honest
- d) All of the above

✅ **Accepted Answer:** d) All of the above

---

## 8. ‘Only he can explain the lecture’. Here, ‘only’ refers to the lecture.

- a) True
- b) False

✅ **Accepted Answer:** b) False

---

## 9. ‘Environmentally speaking, the industry being set up in a biologically dense zone will certainly have an adverse impact.’ This sentence has ___ adverbs.

- a) 0
- b) 1
- c) 2
- d) 3

✅ **Accepted Answer:** d) 3

---

## 10. ‘He is extremely talented’, here the word ‘extremely’ is ___.

- a) Adjective
- b) Degree adverb
- c) Verb
- d) Preposition

✅ **Accepted Answer:** b) Degree adverb
